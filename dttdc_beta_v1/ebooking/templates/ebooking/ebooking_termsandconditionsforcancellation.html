Terms & Conditions for Cancellation
  1. 18% GST will be levied on the applicable cancellation amount.
  2. If cancellation is made 15 days before the date of departure (excluding the departure date), a minimum charge of Rs. 100/- per person or 10% of the package cost, whichever is higher, shall be deducted.
  3. If cancellation is made between 8 to 14 days before the date of departure (excluding the departure date), 25% of the total package cost shall be deducted.
  4. If cancellation is made between 04 to 07 days before the date of departure (excluding the departure date), 50% of the total package cost shall be deducted.
  5. If cancellation is made less than 04 days before the date of departure, 100% of the package cost shall be deducted and no refund shall be admissible.
  6. No refund will be allowed if the tourist misses the tour or cancels the journey after the departure of the tour.
  7. For cancellations made through the online portal, refunds shall be processed strictly as per the applicable cancellation rules.
  8. The eligible refund amount shall be credited to the same bank account or payment mode through which the original payment was made, within a period of 15 days.
  9. In case the tour is cancelled by DTTDC, the tourist may collect the cash refund from the Central Reservation Office or receive the refund online within 15 days.
  10. These cancellation rules are applicable to Regular Tours, LTC Tours, as well as Car Rental services.
