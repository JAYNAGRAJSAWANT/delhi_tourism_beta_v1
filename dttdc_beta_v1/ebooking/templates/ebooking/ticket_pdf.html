Cancellation Rules:

• 18% GST will be levied on the Cancellation amount.

• 15 days before (Excluding departure date), Rs. 100/- per person to be charged as minimum amount or 10% whichever is higher.

• 8-14 days before (Excluding departure date) 25% of the package cost.

• 04-07 days before (Excluding departure date) 50% of the package cost.

• Less than 04 days before departure date 100% of the package cost means no refund.

• No refund will be allowed, if the tourist misses the tour or cancels the journey after the departure of the tour.

• For cancellations made online, refund of money will be as per rules. The refund amount will be send in your account through which the payment has been made within 15 days.

• In case tour is cancelled by DTTDC the tourist collect cash refund from Central Reservation Office or by online within 15 days. These rules will be applicable both for Regular, LTC tours as well as Car Rental
